## Description
A collection of projects I created over a course of 6 months as I explored different methods of simulating fluid dynamics.

## Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics
Engineered a real-time smoothed particle hydrodynamics simulation with raymarched rendering techniques and real-time collision. Wrote a SPH **compute shader** in **HLSL**, integrating it into Unity's **Universal Render Pipeline**.
